Our client, Rentadog AS, is a dog shelter company introduced to us through the CEO Lars Kjøpmann.

Purpose and Goals

Hensikten til «Rent a dog» er å gi mennesker som er glad i hunder muligheten til leie en hund i en hvis tidsperiode. Måten vi skal gjøre det på er at nettsiden skal ha en oversikt over hundene som er mulig å leie og en tydelig oversikt over hvilke hunder som er ledig eller utleid. Oversikten skal ha mange søte bilder og informasjon om hver enkelt hund for å trekke til seg mest mulig kunder. Målet til «Rent a dog» er å omsette for 1 banan i første kvartal av 2023, samtidig som vi skal bli de største i Norge innenfor utleie av hund.

One of Rentadog's dogs

Audience

The audience of “rentadog” is everyone with a love for dogs. Does your kid want a dog? Rentadog is the perfect answer. Your kid will get the dog experience as many times as he likes, while you don’t have to care about the housing of the dog. Are you in need of a date idea for a tinder match? With rentadog you can have a cute puppy at your disposal for a couple of hours. With a Labrador by your side, you will definitely get the girl of your dreams! The answer is that rentadog is for everyone. The perfect match for a dog shelter with loads of food and housing, and people who are looking for a dog, don’t have enough time for a dog or just like to go for walks and want to help others. The audience for rentadog is huge.

Content of the site and how it is organized

The homepage will contain three sections:

  1. A section explaining what the website is
  2. Three emphasized rubrics
  3. A feelgood dog video

At the top of each webpage there will be a menubar containing four elements: An icon linking to the homepage, rubrics, contact us and about us. The content of the rubrics page will naturaly be rubrics. Contact us will be a form for any enquiries towards the firm. About us will contain some basic information about Rentadog AS.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

Functional Requirements

  1. Each dog have a personal page to read about the dog and show available times.
  2. You can book a dog through the website
  3. Every page is accessible

Non-Functional Requirements

  1. Every reference page is only 3 click away from the home page.
  2. The home page is only 1 click away at all times.
  3. Every page should load in less than 1 second.
